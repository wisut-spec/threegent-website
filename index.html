About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Industrial Mineral Trading

Three Gent Intertrade is a new-generation mineral trading company built on drive, transparency, and a genuine passion for connecting quality raw materials with the industries that need them most.

We are not limited by geography, industry, or scale. Whether you are a growing SME or an established manufacturer, we are here to find the right mineral, in the right grade, at a price that works for you.

Young, agile, and always open to new opportunities — we adapt fast, communicate directly, and treat every client relationship as a long-term partnership worth building from day one.
