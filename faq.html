Products & Specifications

Gypsum (Calcium Sulfate) is a naturally occurring mineral widely used as a raw material in the production of tiles, ceramics, plasterboard, and cement. It is also used in agriculture as a soil amendment. Three Gent sources quality Gypsum from licensed, permitted mines.

Dolomite is a calcium-magnesium carbonate mineral commonly used as a flux in steelmaking, a soil conditioner in agriculture, and a raw material in construction. We offer both high-purity and standard grades to suit different applications.

Kaolin (white clay) is a naturally occurring clay mineral widely used in ceramics, paper, and various industrial processes requiring fine white clay. Contact our team to enquire about available grades and specifications that match your application.

Our core products are Gypsum, Dolomite, and Kaolin. We also source additional industrial minerals including Limestone, Quicklime, and Perlite. See the More Minerals Available section on our Products page for the full list, or contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Ordering & Shipping

Yes. Three Gent Intertrade ships internationally on FOB terms from Thailand, with primary loading ports at Laem Chabang Port and Klong Toey Port. Contact us with your destination country and required volume and we will prepare a shipping quote for you.

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the product and destination country. Please contact our team with your specific requirements and we will confirm availability and the applicable minimum for your order.

Documents & Quality

You can request a COA (Certificate of Analysis), TDS (Technical Data Sheet), or Spec Sheet by contacting our team via the enquiry form, LINE, or email. Documents are prepared per production lot and issued in conjunction with your order.

Our minerals are analyzed for chemical composition and purity using standard laboratory methods including XRF and ICP. Testing can be conducted through accredited laboratories in Thailand, such as those certified by the Department of Science Service (DSS) or other recognized bodies. A Certificate of Analysis (COA) is available upon request for each shipment.

Sample analysis data for Gypsum, Dolomite, and Kaolin is available on our Products page.
